Use of cookies

A cookie refers to a file that is sent for the purpose of requesting permission to be stored on your computer. By accepting the cookies policy, such a file is created and the cookie then serves to obtain information regarding web traffic, and also facilitates navigability on future recurring visits to a website. Another function that the cookies have is that with them the web can recognize you individually and therefore provide you with the best personalized service.

Our website does not use cookies in order to identify which pages are visited and their frequency. You can delete cookies at any time from your computer. However, cookies help to provide a better service on websites, they do not give access to information about your computer or you personally, unless you choose to provide it directly. You can accept or deny the use of cookies in the web notice that informs about it, however most browsers accept cookies automatically, as they serve to obtain a better web service. You can also change your computer settings to decline cookies, although in this case you may not be able to use some of our services.
