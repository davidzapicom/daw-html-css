Where is it?

White Desert Whichaway Camp is a unique camp located in the interior of Antarctica. After a five-hour flight across the Southern Ocean, you will be transported to another world. A luxurious and eco-friendly camp located in one of the last great wilderness areas on Earth. Land in Antarctica and transfer to your luxurious eco-friendly camp in a 30-minute flight: all emissions from flights to, from and while on the continent are offset through fully accredited carbon neutral systems.
