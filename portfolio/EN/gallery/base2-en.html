without foundations



Accommodations at Wolf's Fang are in seven heated and insulated tents, which offer guests the opportunity to experience a taste of old-world explorer life in 21st century luxury. In addition, the camp is temporary and is built without foundations or cement, so it can be dismantled, overhauled or upgraded without a trace. In addition, all waste is transported out of Antarctica at the end of the season.
